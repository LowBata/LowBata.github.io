Dross Level 1 Screenplay

Intro

Once upon a time, just beyond the coastal vastness of the deep blue, lies many undersea villages
And in these ocean villages are the fantastical Fish Folk.
Fish folk are Human-like people who evolved from their aquatic ancestors
Not just fish too, the various sea inhabitants have also grown and evolved as well
The many creatures of the sea and Fish Folk have lived in harmony for many millennia, peacefully minding their own business away from the surface dwellers

In the humble town of Umi Village is a local hermit crab named Dross
Now, you see here, Dross is kind of a lazy bum, often considered worthless among the Fish Folk of the village
However, he loves collecting useless trinkets from the surface world.
One peaceful day, a bunch of mysterious objects fell from above, polluting and damaging the surrounding areas of Umi Village
Seeing this as a chance to look for potential treasure among the heap
Dross grabs his metal detector from one of his trips on the surface, using it to detect stuff such as metal and plastic (don’t question it)
Dross now embarks on his quest to hopefully find something treasure worthy and get rich.
So he can finally get the praise and recognition he deserves.
